Eclipse of Grace: The Descent of the Fallen Angel| by Jhon Louie C. San Jose

The fallen angel, her once-glorious wings now tattered, is a mesmerizing figure caught between two worlds. Her ethereal visage is juxtaposed against the backdrop of a muted landscape, symbolizing the stark contrast between the divine realm she left behind and the earthly realm she now inhabits. Beside her, two birds perch as steadfast companions, representing a unique duality of her natureâ€”both graceful and enigmatic.


"Eclipse of Grace" captures the essence of transformation and the journey from divine to mortal. It is a visual tapestry that explores the intricacies of fallen angels, their resilience, and the opportunity for rebirth in the embrace of the earthly realm. Through this artwork, the viewer is invited to witness the poignant moment when the fallen angel grapples with her past, her present, and the path she will forge into the unknown future.
