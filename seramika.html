Maria Sermakia: Filipino Artist Guild Official Character | by Jhon Louie C. San Jose

Maria Seramika, an enchanting embodiment of creativity and Filipino artistic heritage, brought to life by the skillful hands of the Filipino Artist Guild. With a canvas of imagination as her realm, Maria Seramika emerges as a captivating muse who weaves together the threads of tradition and innovation. Clad in a gown that seems to shimmer with strokes of paint and whispers of inspiration, she symbolizes the harmonious blend of the old and the new in the world of art.


Maria Seramika's gaze is a portal to a realm where colors dance and ideas take flight. Her eyes hold the stories of countless strokes, brush meetings with canvas, and the symphony of artistic expression. Her hair, like cascading ink, flows with a dynamic energy that echoes the ebb and flow of the creative process itself. Around her, a tapestry of vibrant hues and abstract forms unfurls, mirroring the diverse artistic voices that form the tapestry of the guild she represents.
